    How It All Happened...

    Started in 2008 by a few friends that wanted to a chase a dream. After visiting some of the best pubs and restaurants across the states we knew we needed to bring it home. Located conveniently in the central area of Jacksonville, FL. The Honey Willow will be your next stop for a few beers and a burger.

    ...A Place For Friends

    Few and far between can be used to describe the handful of restaurants friends can gather to enjoy each others company without being rushed or feeling like a burden. We have decided that we would honor the occasion by providing ample seating and outside patio space so you dont have to leave...ever! Hold On... We do have to close sometime. 1 a.m!
